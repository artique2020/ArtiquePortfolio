Expertise

Our expert team at Artique Fashion Boutique uniquely creates versatile outfits that amalgamates traditional ethnic look with a global touch that helps our customers achieve a quaint yet chic look adding a modern and classy look incorporating all your iconic fashion goals.

Tailoring

Tailor to perfection is our fashion mantra.Our bespoke services starting with a custom design, precise measurements collaborated with modern stitching and a tinge of master craftsmanship always results in stellar ensembles for our customers.

Designing

Our expert and professional designers can help you transform all your creative fashion and stylish ideas that you have ever dreamt of into intricately crafted unique outfits as you create a sartorial elegant identity of your own.

Embroidery

We at Artique Fashion Boutique can weave magic into fabric using our hand embroidery techniques with a spectrum of fascinating coloured threads which can accentuate your wardrobe collections to the next level.

Designer Collections

Our designs are influenced by the culture and social attiude of our neighbourhood. We create designs that out fuctional and comfortable, and use fabric locally available. We at Artique, give importance to the fit and finish of the dresses. We are mainly specialized in kurtis,lehangas, blouses, gowns and kids dresses.
